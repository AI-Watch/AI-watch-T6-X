Data & Analytics Framework (DAF)

Responsible organisation: Agenzia per l'Italia Digitale (Governmental)

The Data & Analytics Framework (DAF) has the goal of improving and simplifying the interoperability and exchange of data between Public Administrations, promoting and improving the management and usage of Open Data, optimizing activities of analysis and knowledge generation.
