Chatbot for citizen general service enquiries

Responsible organisation: Government (Governmental)

The municipality of Frederiksberg launched an AI-based chatbot ("Kiri") to answer citizen queries about local services.. When you visit the Frederiksberg Municipality's website you will be able to get help from the chat bot Kiri. It is a digital assistant to whom citizens can ask general questions and answer immediately. The offer is a trial conducted by Gentofte, Gladsaxe, Lyngby-Taarbæk, Rudersdal, Roskilde and Frederiksberg municipalities, and which is inspired by similar trials with good results in Norway. Initially, Kiri is an attempt, and initially Kiri will only be able to answer questions in the field of citizen services, such as passports and driver's licenses. Kiri is unable to answer specific cases, but will be able to answer the most typical and general questions.
